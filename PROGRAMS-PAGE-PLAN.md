# Superthoughts Programs Page – Plan

## The Problem to Solve

You have one program now but want to build more. The page needs to look complete and professional with one offering, then scale naturally as you add two, three, ten more. It should never look empty.

---

## Page Structure

### 1. Hero Section

**Headline:** "Guided programs for the life you want to live."

**Subline:** Short, grounded description. Something like:
"Each Superthoughts program is a series of guided recordings designed to help you build real, lasting change. No fluff. No filler. Just practice."

**No CTA button here.** The page itself is the menu. Let them scroll and choose.

---

### 2. Featured Program (your current best/only one)

This is a large, prominent card that takes up the full width. It gets special treatment because it's your flagship. When you have more programs, your newest or most popular one stays here.

**Layout:** Image on one side, copy on the other (or full-width with overlay)

**Content:**
- Tag: "Featured Program"
- Title: "Mindful Eating"
- One-line description: "Realign your relationship with food through seven guided sessions."
- 2-3 bullet points:
  - 7 guided recordings (95 minutes)
  - Lifetime access, one-time purchase
  - Trusted by 37,500+ listeners
- Button: "Learn more" → links to mindful-eating.superthoughts.com
- A single testimonial quote beneath it (Natalie S. or Cher M.)

**Why this works with one program:** A single featured card with generous whitespace looks intentional and premium, not empty. Think Apple product pages – one thing, presented beautifully.

---

### 3. "Coming Soon" Row (placeholder for future programs)

This is the key to making the page look complete right now. Below the featured program, add a section that signals more is on the way.

**Section headline:** "More programs in development"

**Layout:** 2-3 cards in a row, each one a teaser:

**Card 1 – Sleep & Rest**
- Tag: "Coming soon"
- Title: "Restful Sleep"
- Description: "A guided practice for letting go of the day and settling into deep, restorative sleep."
- No button, just the card. Maybe a subtle "Join the waitlist" text link.

**Card 2 – Abundance & Mindset**
- Tag: "Coming soon"
- Title: "The Abundance Practice"
- Description: "Shift your relationship with money, success, and self-worth through daily guided sessions."
- Same treatment.

**Card 3 (optional) – Stress & Nervous System**
- Tag: "Coming soon"
- Title: "Nervous System Reset"
- Description: "A practice for calming your nervous system and building resilience to daily stress."

**Why this works:** It makes the page look like a real catalog. It signals that Superthoughts is a growing platform, not a one-off product. It builds anticipation. And when you're ready to launch the next one, you just swap "Coming soon" for a real link.

**Bonus:** Each "Coming soon" card can have a small email capture: "Get notified when this launches." That builds your list for each future program before you even create it.

---

### 4. The Approach (brief section)

A short section that explains what makes a Superthoughts program different. This lives below the program cards and reinforces your brand.

**Headline:** "How every program is built"

Three short points, side by side:

**Practice, not theory**
"Every program is a series of guided recordings you listen to and practice with. No courses to sit through. No homework. Just close your eyes and follow along."

**Built to last**
"These aren't quick fixes. Each program builds a skill through repetition and awareness. The kind of change that stays with you."

**Your pace, your terms**
"No deadlines, no schedules. Listen once or come back daily. The practice meets you where you are."

---

### 5. Social Proof Strip

A simple horizontal row of proof points:

- "37,500+ listens worldwide"
- "4.9 out of 5 average rating"
- "Listeners in 30+ countries"
- A short quote: "Josh is one of my favorite teachers." – Khurty R.

---

### 6. Final CTA

**Headline:** "Start with Mindful Eating"

**Subline:** "Your first step into the Superthoughts practice."

**Button:** "Begin the program" → mindful-eating.superthoughts.com

This anchors the bottom of the page and gives a clear next step.

---

## How This Scales

### With 1 program (now):
- Featured card (Mindful Eating)
- 2-3 "Coming soon" teasers
- Looks intentional and premium

### With 2 programs:
- Featured card (newest program)
- Second program gets its own full card below
- 1-2 "Coming soon" teasers remain

### With 3+ programs:
- Featured card rotates to newest/best seller
- All others in a grid (2 or 3 columns)
- "Coming soon" section removed or kept for future teasers
- Optionally add category filters: "Mindfulness" / "Abundance" / "Sleep"

---

## Squarespace Implementation Notes

You can build all of this in Squarespace without custom code:

- **Featured program:** Use a full-width section with an image block + text block side by side
- **Program cards:** Use the "Card" or "List" section type in Squarespace 7.1
- **Coming soon cards:** Same card layout, just different styling (muted colors, no button)
- **Social proof strip:** Use a "Marquee" or simple text columns section
- **Final CTA:** A banner section with centered text and button

Keep the Squarespace design minimal. Dark background to match your Superthoughts brand. No busy templates. Let the programs be the focus.

---

## Navigation Structure for superthoughts.com

Keep it simple:

| Nav Item | What it is |
|----------|-----------|
| Home | Brand story, who you are, what Superthoughts is about |
| Programs | This page – the catalog |
| About | Your background, your approach, why you do this |
| Blog | Content for SEO and organic traffic (optional, add later) |
| Listen | Link to your complimentary session funnel (mindful-eating.superthoughts.com) |

The "Listen" nav item is your primary CTA in the navigation. It takes people directly into the funnel. Everything else supports that.

---

## What to Build First

1. Deploy the Mindful Eating funnel to Netlify
2. Set up the subdomain (mindful-eating.superthoughts.com)
3. Build the Programs page on Squarespace using this plan
4. Update your Squarespace homepage to link to Programs
5. Add the "Listen" nav CTA pointing to the funnel

You now have a professional brand hub with a scalable course catalog and a high-converting standalone funnel. That's the same architecture the best creators use.
