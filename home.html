The method

Three shifts that change everything.

01

Awareness Before Action

Most eating happens on autopilot. The first shift is simply learning to pause – to notice the impulse before you act on it. Not to judge it. Just to see it. That gap between impulse and action is where everything changes.

02

Sensation Over Story

Your mind tells stories about food: good, bad, guilty, deserved. These recordings train you to drop the narrative and return to what's actually happening in your body. Real hunger feels different from boredom, stress, or habit.

03

Practice Over Perfection

This isn't about getting it right. It's about building a quiet, consistent awareness that compounds over time. Like any skill, the more you practice, the more natural it becomes. There's no failing here. Only noticing.
