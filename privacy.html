Privacy Policy

Superthoughts ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

What we collect

How we use your information

  • To deliver program content you requested or purchased

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Email communication

When you sign up, you will receive emails related to the programs you expressed interest in. Every email includes an unsubscribe link. You can opt out at any time, and we will promptly honor your request.

Third-party services

Each service has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

Cookies

This site uses minimal cookies necessary for functionality. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party trackers.

Data security

We use HTTPS encryption across our entire site. Payment processing is handled by Stripe, which is PCI-DSS compliant. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, though no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us through the website. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Children's privacy

This site and our programs are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
